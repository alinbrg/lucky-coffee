About us

Welcome to Lucky Coffee - Green Beans Trading!

Discover the Finest Green Coffee Beans for Your Roastery

At Lucky Coffee, we take pride in offering premium green coffee beans sourced from the world's finest coffee-growing regions. Whether you're a seasoned roaster or a passionate coffee enthusiast, our extensive selection of high-quality beans will satisfy your discerning palate and elevate your coffee creations to new heights.

Why to choose Lucky Coffee?

Exceptional Quality: We meticulously select and source our green coffee beans from renowned coffee farms across the globe. Our beans undergo rigorous quality control measures to ensure freshness, consistency, and exceptional flavor profiles.

Global Variety: Indulge in a diverse range of coffee origins, each with its own unique characteristics. From the fruity notes of Ethiopian beans to the full-bodied richness of Brazilian coffee, we have the perfect beans to cater to every taste preference.

Direct Trade Relationships: We prioritize ethical sourcing by establishing direct trade relationships with coffee farmers. By eliminating unnecessary intermediaries, we ensure that our farmers receive fair compensation for their hard work, while also fostering sustainable farming practices.

Tailored Orders: We understand that every roastery has its specific requirements. That's why we offer flexible ordering options to cater to your business needs. Whether you need small quantities for experimentation or bulk orders for large-scale production, we've got you covered.

Expert Support: Our team of coffee experts is dedicated to providing exceptional customer service. We are here to assist you at every step, from selecting the perfect beans to offering guidance on roasting techniques and flavor profiles. Your success is our priority.

Convenient Shipping: We make it easy for you to receive your green coffee beans promptly. Our streamlined shipping process ensures that your orders are carefully packed and swiftly delivered to your doorstep, so you can start roasting without delay.

Educational Resources: Expand your coffee knowledge with our professional team. The team can present and share insights, brewing techniques, and industry trends to help you stay ahead of the curve. We believe that education is the key to elevating the coffee experience.
